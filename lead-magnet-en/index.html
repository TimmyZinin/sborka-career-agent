THE CAREER
ANALYSIS PROMPT

Works in ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini. Fill in the [FILL IN] blocks and send.

You are a career consultant with 20 years of experience in the global IT market.
You are also a recruiter who understands how hiring managers think.
You are also a coach who helps people find their strongest market position.

TASK:
Analyze my profile and give me a concrete job search / partnership plan.

MY PROFILE:
- Name: [FILL IN]
- Current role: [e.g. backend developer, product manager, designer]
- Years of experience: [FILL IN]
- Hard skills: [languages, frameworks, tools]
- Soft skills: [what you do beyond your core specialty]
- Industries: [fintech, e-commerce, edtech, etc.]
- Looking for: [employment / freelance / co-founder / partnership]
- Work format: [remote / office / hybrid]
- Salary expectations: [FILL IN or "market rate"]
- What I DON'T want: [dealbreakers]

WHAT I WANT (give me ALL of these in order):

1. TARGET ROLES (3-5)
   For each:
   - Role title
   - Where to find it (specific platforms, channels, sites)
   - Average salary range
   - My fit score (0-10) with explanation
   - What to improve to reach 10/10

2. EMPLOYER PORTRAIT
   - Who are these people (company size, stage, industry)
   - What's their pain point (what problem do I solve)
   - What they value most
   - Red flags to watch for

3. SEARCH STRATEGY
   - TOP 5 platforms with priorities
   - Search queries (copy-paste ready for LinkedIn, Indeed, Telegram)
   - How many applications per day for results
   - Timeline: when to expect first interviews

4. OUTREACH TEMPLATE
   - Cold message for each role (3-4 sentences)
   - What to customize per listing

5. QUICK WINS (5 actions for today)
   - What to do right now, in 1 hour, no preparation needed

Format: structured, with headers, no fluff.
If anything is missing from my profile, ask before answering.
